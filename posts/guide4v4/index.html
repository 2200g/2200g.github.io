4v4 Bedwars Guide.⌗

A guide on 4v4 Bedwars. (If you still haven’t figured it out from the title already)

So you want to sweat 4v4 eh? Don’t know a good strat tho? Well, here you go :D

Make sure to follow your tasks. You’ll prolly messup the match if you do not do so.

Tasks:⌗

Bridger (1st Rusher)⌗

Task: Bridging.

  • Waits for 24-28 Iron.
  • Throws Gold at the 3rd Rusher.
  • Gets Wool. (FFS do not bridge w/ endstone/clay/glass)
  • 3/2 Stacks. (Go high, do NOT onestack.)
Shears (2nd Rusher)⌗

Task: Getting Shears.

  • Waits for 36+12 Iron.
  • Throws 12 Iron and 8 Gold at the Defender.
  • Gets a stack of Wool and Shears.
  • Follows the Bridger.
Tools (3rd Rusher)⌗

Task: Getting tools.

  • Waits for 36/40/44 Iron.
  • Throws Gold at the 3rd Rusher.
  • Gets a Wooden Pickaxe and a Wooden Axe.
  • Gets a single stack of Wool, and Ladders.
  • Follows the Bridger (Bridges if they fall off).
  • Waits for TnT if there are people close enough to hit them off while breaking the defense.
Defender / TnT⌗

Task: Defends and gets TnT.

  • Waits for 76 Iron / 4 Gold 48 Iron.
  • Gets: EndStone and 16 Glass (If: 76 Iron) OR 16 Wood and 16 Glass (If: 4 Gold / 48 Iron)
  • Places down the Butterfly Defense (Wood / EndStone)
  • Reinforces w/ Wood or Wool. (Optional, you probably won’t need to do this often)
  • Helps the Rushers.

Please do note that the only way you’re ever really going to get good at the game, is by actually playing it instead of reading a guide that a random guy on the internet made :P
